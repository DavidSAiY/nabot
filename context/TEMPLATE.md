# Context Template

Fill this out to teach the system how you sound, who you write for, and what you do. This is the source of truth for all content generation.

You can keep everything in this single file, or split sections into separate files if they get large. The system reads everything in `/context/`.

---

## Voice

How you actually sound. Not how you want to sound. Pull from your real writing.

### Core identity
<!-- Who are you as a communicator? Not your job title. -->

### Personality
<!-- 3-5 words that describe your vibe. Example: skeptical, direct, amused, pragmatic -->

### Tone
<!-- The feel of your writing. How formal (1-10)? -->

### How you build arguments
<!-- Do you lead with a thesis? Build from observation? Use stories? -->

### Sentence patterns
<!-- Short and punchy? Long and flowing? Mixed? Any recurring structures? -->

### Signature phrases
<!-- Words and phrases you naturally overuse. Pull from your actual content. -->

### What you never sound like
<!-- This prevents generic AI output. Be specific. Example: motivational speaker, corporate PR, academic hedging -->

### Language
<!-- Primary language, code-switching patterns, bilingual habits -->

### Examples
<!-- Paste 2-3 short excerpts that are unmistakably you. Explain why each one captures your voice. -->

---

## Audience

The specific person you're writing for. The more specific, the better the content.

### Who they are
<!-- Job titles, industries, experience level, geography -->

### What they struggle with
<!-- Real pain points in their own words. Not your marketing version. -->

### What they want
<!-- Short-term and long-term goals. Dream outcome. -->

### How they talk
<!-- Phrases they use to describe their problems. Words that resonate vs. turn them off. -->

### What stops them
<!-- Objections, fears, excuses. What do they need to believe before acting? -->

### Where they hang out
<!-- Platforms, communities, content preferences -->

---

## Business

What you offer and how you talk about it. Keeps CTAs and mentions accurate.

### What you do
<!-- Business name, what you offer, who you serve -->

### Your angle
<!-- What makes you different. Your philosophy. -->

### Offerings
<!-- Products, services, programs. Include pricing if relevant. -->

### Topics you cover
<!-- Main themes and subtopics. Also: what you avoid. -->

### How you promote
<!-- Casual? Direct? Subtle? Your CTA style. -->

### Links
<!-- Website, newsletter, social profiles, product pages -->
